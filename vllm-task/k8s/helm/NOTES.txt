Access:
- vLLM API:        http://localhost:8000/
  * health/models: http://localhost:8000/v1/models
- Streamlit UI:    http://localhost:8000{{ .Values.ingress.streamlitPath }}

The chart installs ingress-nginx as a dependency and binds NodePort 8000 (HTTP) and 8443 (HTTPS).
Update model/dtype/replicas/resources via --set / values.yaml and helm upgrade.
